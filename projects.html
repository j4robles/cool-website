Bonzi Warden


About

Initially built for myself, Bonzi Warden is the ultimate tool to fight procrastination. The stress of deadlines is what motivates people to do the things they don't like, so I took those deadlines and pushed them further. Your helpful Bonzi Buddy will scrape your favorite school site to find what assignments you have to do and give you deadlines beforehand, so as to trigger that stress mechanism in your brain and get you started earlier! In order to ensure that stress is real, Bonzi will be sure to remind you incessantly as the early deadline approaches through helpful popups in the spirit of Bonzi Buddy. Failure to complete your assignment on time will result in the compromisation of your device for up to 24 hours through a barrage of popups dense and insistent enough to inhibit computer usage. To ensure you can't simply Task Manager your way out, Bonzi creates concurrent services and watchdog instances to be real tricky to disable for the average computer layman. Some procrastinators require more than the threat of a few points lost to get the motivation they need to be productive! Malware? Never heard of it.

Featured Technologies

  • C# — Primary language for interface, logic, data management, and web scraping

  • C++ — Language for dumb popup instances created by the warden, employs system calls to self-replicate and annoy the user into submission

  • SQLite — To store local user data

HashVar


About

Have you ever needed to generate infinitely many variations of a PNG or JPEG or WEBP? Probably not, but it's great if you want to annoy people on imageboards by spamming the same image in a way that can't be hash filtered. HashVar gives options to vary a given image in a multitude of ways to vary the hash while preserving the high-level view of the original image, through randomizing various metadata attributes, changing the dimensions in small but random amounts, and random pixel manipulation. Uses multi-threading so you can generate thousands of variations of the same image even faster.

Featured Technologies

  • C++ — Primary language for the entire program, used with multithreading to speed up generation
